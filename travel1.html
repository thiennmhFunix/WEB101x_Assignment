About Travel

Generous natural conditions and a submerged inland ecosystem have awarded Dong Thap with multiple valuable ecotourism resources, for example, Tram Chim National Park, known both locally and internationally. This is a miniature ecological model of Dong Thap Muoi and the habitat of red-head cranes - a precious bird species protected by the world. Tram Chim National Park also achieves 7 out of 9 criteria in the Ramsar list of wetlands of international importance. Also of importance is the Gao Giong ecotourism site.

Situated by the bank of the Tien River, the flower village of Sa Dec is the homeland of hundreds of "uncanny flowers and strange herbs". The village - one of the flower centers in the south of Vietnam - covers an area of around 300 ha for planting flowers and ornamental trees.

Enjoying nature by having meal in place surrounded by lotus on a lake is best thing that visitors should not ignore.

City night life is amazing and colorful. There are a lot of food on the street. There is also many kind of activities in the big square of Cao Lanh city.

Nguyen Sinh Sac tomb is where people adore and tribute the contribute of Nguyen Sinh Sac who had been contributed greatly to the country. Here visitors could explore more about the life of people in the past.

There are a lot of visitors coming to Dong Thap every year. Foreign visitors have interested in taking a tour around the city in special local traditional vehicle.
